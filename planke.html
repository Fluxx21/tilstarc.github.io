The Challenge

People live in houses or flats of varying sizes,
which means they require tables of different sizes.
However, not all stores carry tables that fit everyone's needs.

Solution

Our team created a web configurator that allows you to create the table of your desire.

There are over 20 different options to choose from. Choose from materials, height, length, width, and thickness.

After creating the table of your dreams, have it delivered and assembled in your home.
