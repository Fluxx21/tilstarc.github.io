The Challenge

Design and develop a website for an electric motor company,
while ensuring that users can easily complete a conversion.

Solution

I designed a minimalistic and user-friendly design that prioritizes clear navigation and easy access to conversion points such as "Contact Us" buttons.

Used simple language and visuals in product descriptions and features to help users make informed decisions and complete conversions.

Then optimized the website for all devices to ensure that users have a seamless experience regardless of how they choose to access the website.
